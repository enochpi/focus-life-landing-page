Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: April 2026 · SpartaLabs

Short version: Seven Seconds does not collect, store, or share any of your data. Download it, play it, that's it.

1. Who we are

Seven Seconds is developed and maintained by SpartaLabs. Questions? Email us at hi@spartalabs.app.

2. Data we do NOT collect

Seven Seconds does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Any data from third-party services

3. How the app works

Seven Seconds is a fully offline timer game. You tap seven times and the game runs entirely on your device — nothing is ever sent to a server. There is no account, no sign-in, and no internet connection required.

4. App permissions

Seven Seconds does not request any special device permissions. It requires no access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data.

5. Third-party services

Seven Seconds contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app works fully offline.

6. Children's privacy

Seven Seconds is appropriate for all ages. Because the app collects absolutely no data from any user, it is fully safe for children under 13 and compliant with COPPA and similar children's privacy laws.

7. Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. We will never introduce data collection without clearly notifying users first.
