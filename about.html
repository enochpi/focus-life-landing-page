About Focus Life 🌱

Built by a solo dev · SpartaLabs · Building until college 🚀

Why I built this

I kept watching people around me — constantly on their phones, scrolling for hours, not really doing anything. Just... rotting. And I realized I could either complain about it, or actually build something useful.

So instead of making another mindless game, I built Focus Life — an app that fights back against the scroll. Something that makes putting your phone down feel rewarding instead of boring.

"Everyone's glued to their phone. I wanted to build something that made being productive feel just as good."

Who I am

I'm a young developer building apps under SpartaLabs — my own little software studio. Focus Life is one of my first real apps shipped to the public, and I'm proud of every pixel of it.

I'm still in school, building in my free time, and planning to keep shipping apps for years to come. College is still a few years away — until then, I'm building. 🚀

SpartaLabs is my indie software studio — a one-person operation building apps that are actually useful. The goal is simple: ship things that help people, keep them free, and keep building.

Focus Life is the first app under the SpartaLabs name, but it won't be the last. More tools are in the works. Stay tuned. 🚀
