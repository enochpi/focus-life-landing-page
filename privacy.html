Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: April 2026 · SpartaLabs

We'll keep this simple: Focus Life does not collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device. That's it.

1. Who we are

Focus Life is developed and maintained by SpartaLabs. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at [email protected].

2. Data we do NOT collect

Focus Life does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Payment information
  • Any data from third-party services

3. Data stored on your device

All app data — including your peas, coins, focus history, streaks, achievements, and settings — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is never transmitted to any server.

You can export or delete your save data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

4. App permissions

Focus Life may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications — to send you daily focus reminders and streak alerts. You can disable these at any time in your device settings.
  • Usage Access — only if you enable the app blocking feature. This allows the app to detect which apps are open so it can block distractions during focus sessions. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

5. Third-party services

Focus Life does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app works fully offline and requires no internet connection.

6. Children's privacy

Focus Life does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is nothing to protect.

7. Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly notifying users first.
