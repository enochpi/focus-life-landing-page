FAQ

Common questions. 💬

Is Focus Life free? +
Yes, completely free to download and play. No ads, no paywalls, no subscriptions — free forever! 🎉
What are peas? +
Peas are your focus energy — the in-game currency you earn from completing focus sessions. Convert them into coins to buy upgrades and furniture for your home. 🌱
Does it work offline? +
Yes! Everything runs on your device — no internet connection required after download.
Is it like a Pomodoro timer? +
Similar concept, but way more rewarding! You set your own session length (up to 7 hours) and earn peas based on how long you focus.
What platforms is it on? +
Currently Android only. iOS is on the roadmap! 🍎
Will my progress be saved? +
Yes! Everything is saved locally on your device. You can also export and import your save data from settings.
